The Resort

The Tropic Hotel is situated in a green luxurious environment with a superb view over the sea and 7km away from Madagascar. An ideal ecolodge for nature lovers, the Tropic Lodge*** is 45 minutes away from the airport on the west coast of Sainte Marie.

The Lodge offering a wonderful view of the private cove and the Sainte Marie Canal from your bungalow or the swimming pool.
